FRUIT SMOOTHIE

Description

Fruit smoothie is a refreshing and healthy beverage that combines various fruits, such as strawberries, blueberries, pineapples, and mangoes, to create a delicious, creamy and refreshing drink. The smoothie is made by blending the juices of these fruits, typically at room temperature, and then adding a small amount of water. The smoothie is then stirred to combine the flavors and textures of the ingredients. Fruit smoothie is a great choice for those looking for a healthy and satisfying snack, especially during a busy lifestyle.

Ingredients

for 2 servings

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 cup milk or almons milk
  • 1 tablesppon honey (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Slice the banana. Measure out the frozen strawberries.
  2. Combine: In a blender, add the banana, frozen strawberries, yogurt, milk, honey, and vanilla extract.
  3. Blend: Blend on high speed until all ingredients are well combined and the mixture is smooth.

Enjoy your refreshing fruit smoothie!
